Artificial Intelligence is a growing ideology in today's society and is taking the internet by
storm. AI (Artificial Intelligence) has grown to be so much more sophisticated and complex
over the last decade, being able to perform human tasks that require logic beyond your average
computer's understanding, such as answering custom questions that you have.

So we now know what AI is and how it works, but how does it affect our daily lives?


Artificial Intelligence poses a threat to our current jobs and employment in general.
This is because most jobs can actually be done by machines/AI, which is what is
slowly happening all over the world.

For example, farming jobs.




Farming jobs are becoming less accessible and endangered due to the threat of machines taking
over the farming industry. As these machines are capable of doing human tasks, there is now no
need for people to do the work, removing the capability of employment.
